How to read this

It's a model, not a mirror

No real feeds are scraped. This is a hand-built model of the incentives — how each platform tends to amplify engagement, outrage, and identity, and how demographic signals steer what surfaces. The point is the shape of the bubble, not any specific post.

The meters

Outrage & fear and engagement-bait track how much of the feed is engineered to provoke rather than inform. Echo shows how much of the political content already agrees with the person — the higher it climbs, the tighter the bubble.

Why the platforms differ

X rewards the angriest quote-tweet; Instagram rewards aspiration; YouTube rewards the next click down the rabbit hole; Facebook rewards the shared article that becomes a town's truth. Same person, six different realities.

Built as an insight tool about filter bubbles and algorithmic amplification. Content is fictional and illustrative. Open source — the whole model lives in docs/data.js.
